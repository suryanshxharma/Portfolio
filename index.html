Key Benchmarks & Pipeline

  • State-of-the-Art Diagnostic Benchmarks: Reached a top-tier 97.91% validation accuracy and a 0.99 micro-average ROC-AUC score across 38 diverse pathological crop classes on the PlantVillage and PlantDoc datasets, outperforming established baseline models and edge architectures such as EdgePlantNet.

Stock Price Prediction

TensorFlow LSTM GRU scikit-learn yfinance

Key Architecture & Features

  • 21 Financial Features: Engineered across trend, momentum, and volume (e.g., RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands) to deeply represent temporal market states.
  • Eliminated Temporal Data Leakage: Enforced strict chronological splits, isolated preprocessing, and leakage-safe sliding-window generation for valid out-of-sample testing.
  • Quantified Model Trade-offs: Compared performance across MAE, RMSE, MAPE, and Directional Accuracy, proving GRUs delivered faster training with fewer parameters without sacrificing accuracy.
